Hidden Rules & Red Flags

Rules that traders often miss in the fine print which can lead to unexpected account breaches.

News Trading Window

Medium

If you have a standard account, executing or closing any trade 2 minutes before or after high-impact news on the affected instrument will result in a violation. Limits orders triggered during this window also count as a breach.

Source: FTMO Trading Objectives, News Trading Rule

Weekend Holding Restrictions

Medium

On Standard accounts, all trades must be closed before the weekend market close (usually Friday 21:00 CE(S)T for Forex). Failing to do so is a hard breach. To hold over weekends, you must purchase the specific 'Swing' account type.

Source: FTMO FAQ, Trading Conditions

Tick Scalping & Arbitrage

High

FTMO explicitly forbids trading styles that exploit platform latency, tick scalping, or arbitrage. Using an EA that executes hundreds of trades for tiny pip gains will lead to account termination without refund.

Source: FTMO Terms & Conditions, Prohibited Trading Practices

Who is This Firm Best For?

Best for

Not suitable for
